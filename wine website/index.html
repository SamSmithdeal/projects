How to taste wine

Process

Sight

Start by observing the color and clarity of the wine. This can give hints to the age and acidity level of the wine.

Smell

Next you want to smell the wine. Start by swirling the wine in the glass to help release the aromas. Place your nose directly above the wine and take a quick sniff. If the aroma is not noticeable from a distance further than a few inches than the wine is likely cheap.

Taste

To properly taste the wine a few steps must be taken. Begin by taking a small sip. before you swallow it is important to swirl the wine in your mouth lightly and inhale a small amount. Since most of the taste from wine comes from the nose it is important to have airflow while you sip.

Grading

Once finished write down your findings and overall thoughts of the wine. These can be recorded and saved for later on our "Add Wines" page.
